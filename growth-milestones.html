Growth Milestones



Birth to Three

Creative Expression - the ability for children to use their imagination to create something
Social Emotional Devolepment (SEL) - the ability for children to engage in social relationships and to interact
Communication Skills - the ability for children to talk verbally or non-verbally

Three to Four

Early Literacy - the ability for children to communicate, verablly or non-verbally, and listen to others
Learning Approach - the ability for children to engage in expereinces a caregiver or parent teaches them
Early Thinking - the ability for children to make and communicate via sounds and images that show mood or feelings
